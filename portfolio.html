About Me

Hi, I'm Hannah Joy 👋

A UI Designer and Front-End Developer student in the Philippines, focused on building intuitive, responsive, and visually engaging digital experiences.

I specialize in transforming ideas into functional interfaces through a combination of user-centered design, modern front-end development, and thoughtful interaction design. My work bridges creativity and technology — from wireframes and design systems to fully responsive web interfaces.

With a strong attention to detail, I aim to create products that are not only aesthetically refined but also optimized for usability, accessibility, and performance. I enjoy crafting seamless user journeys and turning complex problems into clean, scalable solutions.

My toolkit includes UI prototyping, responsive design, front-end technologies, and design-to-development workflows that ensure consistency across every screen and interaction.

Outside of design and development, I enjoy exploring creative inspiration through coffee ☕, reading, and experimenting with new ideas and technologies.
